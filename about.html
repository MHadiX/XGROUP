Our Methodology

Theoretical knowledge is insufficient in modern cyber warfare. At X GROUP, we operate on a strictly practical paradigm. We work in simulated enterprise environments and tasked with defending infrastructure, hunting active threats, and identifying zero-day vulnerabilities.

01. Assessment

Evaluating baseline knowledge, coding fundamentals, and critical thinking under pressure.

02. Infiltration

Learning the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) of advanced persistent threats.

03. Fortification

Engineering robust defenses, writing custom detection rules, and securing perimeters.
